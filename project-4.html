Local Destination Photoshoot

Local Destination Photoshoot is a recent project from the Image Editing Fundamentals course, focusing on showcasing the beauty of local destinations in Yukon. The project involves creating a photo album (Unedited & Edited photos) using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Lightroom to highlight the stunning scenery and attractions of the area.
